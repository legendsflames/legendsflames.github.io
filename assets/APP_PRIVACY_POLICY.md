## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

**ReBlade does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.** We do not require user accounts, and we do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.

### 2. Device Permissions

The App requests the following permissions to function properly:

#### Camera Permission
- **Purpose:** To record Beyblade battle videos
- **Usage:** Videos are recorded locally on your device
- **Storage:** All recordings are saved to your device's local storage in the "ReBlade Battles" album
- **Data Sharing:** We do not access, transmit, or share your camera data

#### Microphone Permission
- **Purpose:** To record audio for battle videos
- **Usage:** Audio is recorded alongside video during recording sessions
- **Storage:** Audio data remains on your device within the recorded videos
- **Data Sharing:** We do not access, transmit, or share your audio data

#### Media Library/Storage Permission
- **Purpose:** To save recorded videos and access them in the gallery
- **Usage:** 
  - Save videos to your device's "ReBlade Battles" album
  - Access and display videos in the in-app gallery
  - Allow video playback and deletion
- **Storage:** All media remains on your device
- **Data Sharing:** We do not access, transmit, or share your media files
